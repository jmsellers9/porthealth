Submission Guidelines

Step 1: Prepare Documents

Prepare available documents in digital format (PDF, JPG, PNG, DOC, DOCX, XLS, XLSX, CSV). Maximum file size per document is 10MB.

Step 2: Complete Form

Fill in all required vessel information including Vessel Name and Estimated Time of Arrival (ETA). All fields marked with * are mandatory.

Step 3: Upload Documents

Upload at least one required document in the categories below. You can submit with partial documentation if needed.

Step 4: Submit & Confirmation

Review all information and submit. You will receive a submission ID and confirmation email. Processing typically takes 2-4 business hours.
